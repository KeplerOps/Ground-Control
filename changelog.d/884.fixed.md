### Fixed

- `/implement` Step 13 no longer halts the workflow on a clean test-quality
  review, AND no longer echoes findings back to the user when the review
  returns work to do. Step 13 now mirrors Step 6.5's record-keeping
  contract with explicit Case A / Case B branches: on findings the parent
  fixes every finding in the same agent turn (classify, fix, self-verify,
  commit/push, post decision record with dispositions, re-invoke) without
  stopping or surfacing the findings back to the user as a status report;
  on clean the parent posts `findings: []` and advances to Step 14 in the
  same turn after `ok: true`. On `ok: false` the parent follows the
  returned `error`/`next_action`, fixes the underlying tooling issue, and
  retries the post rather than advancing without the durable marker. The
  policy check `run_step13_decision_record_contract` enforces the contract
  structurally: it requires the fix-in-same-turn directive and the
  `ok: true` success precondition, and rejects six anti-contract patterns
  (`skip-decision-record`, `do-not-call-post-record`, `do-not-proceed-step14`,
  `findings-empty-not-enough`, `require-user-acknowledgment-turn`,
  `findings-routed-to-user`) with a 60-char negation-window guard so
  legitimate `do not skip` / `on ok:false, do not advance` prose does not
  false-positive.
