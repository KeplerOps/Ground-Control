### Fixed

- `/implement` Step 13 no longer halts the workflow on a clean test-quality
  review. Step 13 now mirrors Step 6.5's record-keeping contract: every
  cycle ends with `gc_post_decision_record(reviewer: "test-quality",
  findings: [...])`, and a clean cycle's empty-findings record is the
  structured advance-to-Step-14 signal once the call returns `ok: true`.
  On `ok: false` the parent follows the returned `error`/`next_action`,
  fixes the underlying tooling issue, and retries the post rather than
  advancing without the durable marker. A new policy check
  (`run_step13_decision_record_contract`) parses the SKILL's Step 13
  section and prevents the contract — including the success-precondition
  clause — from silently regressing.
